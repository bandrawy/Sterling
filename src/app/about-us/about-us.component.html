Sterling Performance, is owned by Michael J. D’Anniballe and was incorporated under the laws of Michigan, November 12, 1990. The evolution of the enterprise which has become Sterling Performance, Inc. includes the world’s finest, fastest and most reliable Racing Engines that money can buy, as well as one of the best Accredited Testing Laboratories in Michigan.
Located in Milford, Michigan – Sterling Performance provides development, durability, and validation testing of fuel systems and components for automotive, marine, off-road, and industrial manufacturers and suppliers, as well as vibration, environmental, engine dynamometer service, and materials testing. See what other industries we serve.
Sterling Performance, Inc. is currently housed in a 34,000 square foot facility in Milford Michigan with 26 personnel on site, and service centers across the country to better serve all our customers, locally, nationally and internationally
We have the finest most experienced staff, which is why our engines and testing integrity cannot be beat; having led to numerous world speed records and international race wins, as well as patents and top quality tested products for our test service customers.
