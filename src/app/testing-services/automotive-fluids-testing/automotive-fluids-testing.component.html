Automotive Fluids Testing

Fluid Compatibility Testing – Hoses and Components:
We conduct compatibility testing of fluid hoses, fuel lines, and components against various automotive fluids to determine compatibility or effect of exposure.

Exposure Testing to Automotive Fluids via Soaking, Exposing, Spraying:

Various components such as filters, hoses, plastic parts etc. can be soaked, sprayed, or exposed to various fluids to determine compatibility or reaction such as deforming, cracking, swelling, etc.

Some Typical Fluids Include:

  • Hydraulic Fluids
  • Brake Fluid
  • Transmission Fluid
  • Power Steering Fluid
  • Coolants – Engine Coolant
  • Glycols
  • Cleaning Fluids, Engine Cleaning Fluids
  • Solvents
  • Refrigerants
  • Fuels (Gasoline, Diesel, Corrosive, Alternative, etc.)

Examples of Typical Parts Tested:

  • Fuel Lines
  • Lines and Hoses
  • Serpentine Belts
  • Reservoirs
  • Connectors, Clamps, Fasteners
  • Filters
  • Plastic Parts
  • Radiators
